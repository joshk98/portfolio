Latest Works

Cat Simulator

"Cat Simulator" is a video game where players get to play as a cat, customize their appearance, explore different environments, and engage in activities like hunting and playing with toys. The game also includes challenges to complete for advancement.

Zing!

"Zing!" is an application that helps users write witty comebacks to annoying emails. With pre-written snarky responses and a custom insult feature, users can shut down pesky emails with a clever retort. The program also gamifies the experience, awarding points and badges for successful zingers. Say goodbye to boring email replies and hello to a more entertaining inbox with "Zing!"
